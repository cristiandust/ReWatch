ReWatch keeps your streaming progress in sync

Track playback across Netflix, Disney+, HBO Max, Crunchyroll, Plex, and more with detectors built for each platform. ReWatch captures where you left off and makes it effortless to jump back in—all while staying private and local-first.

Get the code Join the beta

Purpose-built detectors

  • Understand each platform’s DOM and playback quirks.
  • Capture titles, seasons, episodes, and movie metadata.
  • Auto-detect the right video element even when ads play.

Reliable progress tracking

  • Heartbeat updates every few seconds while you watch.
  • Instant resume buttons inside the popup dashboard.
  • Export and back up your history whenever you need.

Privacy-first architecture

  • No cloud storage or external analytics.
  • All data stays in Chrome Storage on your device.
  • Transparent codebase with community contributions.
